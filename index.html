What services do you offer?

From general consultations and specialist appointments to emergency support, our team ensures you receive timely medical attention. How can I book an appointment online?

Booking an appointment online is simple and convenient. You can start by visiting our official website or mobile app, where you’ll find a dedicated “Book Appointment” section. From there, select the type of service or specialist you need, choose your preferred date and time, and provide basic details such as your name and contact information.

Do you provide emergency support?

Yes, we do provide emergency support to ensure that you receive immediate care when it matters most. Our emergency services are available 24/7, staffed by experienced medical professionals who are trained to respond quickly to urgent situations. Whether it’s a sudden illness, accident, or any critical health concern, our team is equipped with advanced facilities and resources to deliver prompt treatment.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept a variety of payment methods to make transactions easy and flexible for you. You can pay using major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. We also support digital payment options such as net banking, UPI, and popular mobile wallets for quick and secure processing. For those who prefer traditional methods, cash payments are available at our service counters.

How can I contact customer care?

You can easily reach our customer care team through multiple channels designed for your convenience. The quickest way is by calling our dedicated helpline, where representatives are available to assist you with inquiries, appointments, or support needs. You can also contact us via email or through the “Contact Us” section on our official website, which includes a form for submitting questions or feedback.
